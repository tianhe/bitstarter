Make online dating fun and exciting again. Feel your heart race as you play a game that's more than just a game!

The participants: 1 bachelor(ette), 5 contestants, 25 viewers

Viewers watch as the bachelor(ette) measures up the contestants and narrow them down from 5 to 3 to 1!

Don't feel sorry for the eliminated contestants though, they can stay on to meet new bachelor(ettes) or be contacted by an audience member who's been watching.

How Do I Play?

  • Join or start a live dating game
  • Choose your role: bachelor(ette), contestant, or viewer
  • Wait for others to join
  • Ask questions, find out about each other, and meet your true love in as quickly as 15 minutes!

FAQ

Why would I want to be a bachelor(ette)?

As the bachelor(ette), you have the chance to ask the questions that matter to you.

Oh yeah, did we mention you get to choose from five contestants? The odds have never been so good!

Why would I want to be a contestant?

You get a chance to compete for someone who's caught your eye. Hey, no one ever said love is suppose to be easy!

If you do fail in your noble pursuit, there is a consolation price, an audience member might just swoop in for your attention.

Why would I want to watch other people?

Let me list the ways

  • It's fun to support your friends
  • It's entertaining to watch strangers
  • You might learn a thing or two about courtship
  • You might just find yourself becoming the next contestant or bachelor(ette)
